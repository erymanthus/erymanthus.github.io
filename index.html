Description


Looking for a delicious and nutritious meal to energize you? Got some unfinished bottles of Mountain Dew hanging around and crowding up your desk space? Follow this recipe if you want to be dead by 30. Experience the Dew!



Ingredients


  • one bag of Doritos, finely minced
  • two bags of gummy worms
  • 20 strips of fat juicy bacon
  • your leftover bottles of dew (use multiple flavors for a more refined taste and appearance)
  • Anything else you want! Don't forget the veggies. Those soggy McDonald's fries or some candy corn will do.

    •


Directions



  1. Lay those tempting bacon strips on a baking sheet and pour
    some dew all over them, then shove in the oven.
  2. While the bacon is caramelizing, get your bag of doritos and
    crush them up however you like. If you're feeling a little lightheaded
    from exerting the energy to move to the kitchen, then just put the
    bag on your chair and sit back down.
  3. Dump your crushed doritos into a big pot and add your chosen
    dews and gummy worms. It is very exhausting work to dump things in a
    pot so make sure to refresh yourself with some of the dew when you need it.
  4. Your almost done! Take that sizzling, crispy bacon out of the oven
    and throw it in the pot. Stir everything together and stare into the pot.
    Admire the beauty that your hard work has produced. Marvel at all the
    major food groups united, to create a dazzling rainbow of colors so shockingly
    yet beautifully edible. It's magical isn't it?
